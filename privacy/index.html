Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

JobTiered evaluates job postings directly within your browser. To function, the extension accesses the text of the job descriptions on supported career sites. If you create an account to upgrade your tier, we collect your email address and account credentials. Payment processing information is collected securely by Stripe, and we do not store your full payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

The job description data read by the extension is processed locally to generate your grades and queue. Your email address is used solely for account authentication, subscription management, and providing technical support.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share limited necessary data with third-party service providers, specifically Stripe, strictly for the purpose of processing subscription payments.

4. Data Retention and Security

Job caching data is stored locally in your browser. Account data is retained as long as your account is active. We implement standard security measures to protect your email and account status.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your account information at any time. You can clear your locally cached job data directly through the extension settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please refer to our Help page or contact our support team.
