Service Description

JobTiered is a premium browser extension that leverages AI to evaluate, score, and tier job postings in real-time. By subscribing, users gain access to automated rating metrics directly within their browser to streamline their job search.

Refund Policy

Due to the nature of our service, which instantly incurs non-recoverable computing and third-party API costs each time a job posting is evaluated, we do not offer refunds. All subscription payments are final. If you encounter technical issues preventing the extension from working, please contact support for immediate assistance.

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your JobTiered subscription at any time. Upon cancellation, you will retain full access to all premium features until the end of your current paid billing cycle. We do not prorate cancellations or partial months.

JobTiered is a digital software-as-a-service product. There are no physical goods, return processes, or physical shipping terms. Users must be 18 years of age or older to subscribe.
