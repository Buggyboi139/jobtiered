Level up your job search

Job hunting is draining enough without dealing with hidden salaries, toxic culture red flags, and ghost jobs. JobTiered is a browser extension that saves you time by evaluating and grading job postings before you even click on them.

Instead of making you copy and paste links into a separate app, JobTiered injects simple letter grades (S through F) directly onto the job cards of the sites you already use.

Install Extension

Core Features

  • Instant Grading: Hover over any grade badge to see an estimated salary range, pros, red flags, and a quick summary.
  • Two Evaluation Modes:
    • Objective Mode: Focuses strictly on compensation, transparency, and toxic workplace signals.
    • Personal Fit Mode: Compares the job to your resume and salary needs to see if it actually makes sense for you.
  • The Dimmer: If a job scores a 'D' or 'F' for personal fit, the extension fades it out so you can easily scroll past the junk.
  • Keyword Highlighting: Automatically highlights the skills in the job description that match your resume.
  • Built-in Tracker: Save jobs directly from the search feed into a simple tracking board (Saved, Applied, Offer, Rejected).
  • Application Tools: Generate a tailored cover letter, ATS resume tweaks, and interview prep questions for any saved job with one click.
