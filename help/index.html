Getting Started

After installing JobTiered from the Chrome Web Store, pin the extension to your browser toolbar. Click the icon to configure your profile keywords. Once set up, navigate to any supported career site, and JobTiered will automatically begin evaluating job postings on the page.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't the extension grading jobs on a specific site?

JobTiered relies on the specific structure of supported platforms. If a platform recently updated its layout, the extension may temporarily fail to read the descriptions. Make sure your extension is updated to the latest version.

How do I manage my queue?

Click the extension icon to open the popup interface. Navigate to the "Queue" tab to view, edit, or clear your saved job postings.

How does the grading algorithm work?

The extension compares the requirements in the job description against the keywords and preferences you established during setup, calculating a match percentage based on frequency and context.
