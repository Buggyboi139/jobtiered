Refund Policy

Eligibility for Refunds

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new JobTiered Pro subscriptions. If you are not satisfied with the premium features, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase.

Non-Refundable Circumstances

Refunds will not be granted under the following conditions:

  • Requests made after 14 days from the initial purchase date.
  • Subscription renewals (unless canceled prior to the renewal date).
  • Accounts terminated due to a violation of our Terms of Service.

How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund request, please contact us at support@jobtiered.com using the email address associated with your JobTiered account. Please include your Stripe receipt number in the email. Refund requests are typically processed within 5 to 7 business days.

Processing

Approved refunds will be credited back to the original method of payment used during checkout. Depending on your financial institution, it may take additional days for the funds to appear in your account.
